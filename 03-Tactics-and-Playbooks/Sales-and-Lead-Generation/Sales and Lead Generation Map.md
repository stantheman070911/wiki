## Capacity decision

This folder remains flat because acquisition, discovery, teaching, and closing form one operating journey and many playbooks span several stages. The MOC supplies the extra hierarchy while preserving that end-to-end view. Reconsider at 50 entries or when a stable split between lead generation and sales execution would reduce rather than duplicate placement decisions.

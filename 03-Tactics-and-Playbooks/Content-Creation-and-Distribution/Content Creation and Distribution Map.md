## Capacity decision

This folder has a dedicated MOC because production mechanics, message design, testing, and distribution are distinct retrieval jobs even though they remain one operating system. Keep the folder flat while these four linked groups remain understandable; reconsider physical subfolders when ownership separates or a group exceeds roughly 25 active pages.

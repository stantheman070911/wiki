Use this map for life design, meaningful work, wellbeing, formative relationships, and the human side of leadership and management. Return to [[04-Frameworks-and-Mental-Models/Frameworks and Mental Models Index|Frameworks and Mental Models]].

## Conceptual clusters

- **Life design and meaning:** coherency, wayfinding, choices, moments, and parallel futures
- **Work and career:** motivation, workplace influence, redesign, quitting, and skill-shaped contribution
- **Leadership and management:** maturity, output, feedback, trust, hybrid structures, and coordination
- **Wellbeing:** happiness, presence, flow, acceptance, curiosity, and resilience
- **Relationships and formation:** community, cooperation, parenting, feedback, and shared growth

## Capacity decision

This folder remains flat because life design, work, leadership, and wellbeing repeatedly converge on the same questions of agency, relationship, and meaningful contribution. The MOC provides orientation without pretending those themes are independent silos. Reconsider at 50 entries or when work-and-leadership pages form a stable cluster with a distinct placement rule.

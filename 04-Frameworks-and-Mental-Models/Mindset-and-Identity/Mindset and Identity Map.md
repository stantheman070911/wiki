## Capacity decision

This folder remains flat despite its size because the five clusters overlap heavily and do not yet form stable, exclusive ownership categories. The MOC and topic facets provide orientation without adding a misleading folder axis. Reconsider subfolders at 60 entries or when at least three clusters can be assigned without routine cross-placement disputes.

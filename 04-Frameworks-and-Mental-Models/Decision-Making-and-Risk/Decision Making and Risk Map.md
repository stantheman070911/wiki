## Conceptual clusters

- **Evidence:** believability, track records, independent thinking, triangulation
- **Risk:** ruin, optionality, asymmetric bets, skin in the game
- **Decision process:** pre-mortems, boundary conditions, reversibility, feedback
- **Time:** Lindy effects, repeated exposure, long-term consequences
- **Strategic change:** constraints, change cost, technology, institutional transitions

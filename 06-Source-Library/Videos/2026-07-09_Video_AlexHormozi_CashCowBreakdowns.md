## Net-new signal -> derived entries

**01-Business-Strategy (new)**
- **The SMB Service Barbell - Go Cheap or Go Proven, the Middle Is a Dead Zone** — the $1.5–3k/month SMB-services dead zone; go near-free/automated or go up-market to proven operators.
- **The Direct-Response Doom Loop and the One-Brand Bet** — media-arbitrage ceiling on e-commerce portfolios (~$10–12M/brand); concentrate on the one brand with real brand potential.

**03-Tactics-and-Playbooks (new)**
- **Outbound and Inbound Are Separate Teams - The Sales Career Ladder** — blending self-generated and company-provided leads on the same reps kills outbound hustle; make inbound access an earned promotion.

**04-Frameworks-and-Mental-Models (new)**
- **Solve the Problem You Have, Not the One You're Afraid Of - Metrics Over Narrative** — don't redirect strategy for a feared trend (e.g. AI disruption) until it shows up in an actual business metric.
- **The Greener-Pasture Fallacy - Fix the Business You Have Before You Start the Next One** — starting an unrelated new venture is often an escape from a solvable constraint in the current business, not a genuinely better opportunity.
- **The X-Factor Dilution Problem - Four Ways to Scale Past Yourself** — when the founder's personal skill is the product, the four scaling levers are hire-and-train, productize, reprice-at-fixed-supply, or change the delivery ratio.

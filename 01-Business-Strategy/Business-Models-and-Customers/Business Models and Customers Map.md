## Conceptual clusters

- **Model architecture:** business shapes, recurring revenue, services, software, education, e-commerce
- **Customer choice:** ideal customers, qualification, niches, communities, and smallest viable markets
- **Value delivery:** customer outcomes, activation, retention, churn, and product-led experience
- **Scalability:** productized intellectual property, founder independence, delegation, and network effects
- **Purpose and durability:** human value, philanthropy, defensibility, and long-term model quality

## Capacity decision

This folder remains flat because business-model architecture and customer design are tightly coupled: changing the customer often changes delivery, retention, and economics. The MOC provides stable navigation without forcing pages into narrower folders that would obscure those dependencies. Reconsider at 45 entries or when model architecture and customer-lifecycle work become independently owned editorial areas.
